Chicken with sundried tomatos and feta cheese

Finished dish.

The dish is veery saucy and it's recommended to serve with a pasta of personal preference!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Creme fraiche
  • Chicken stock
  • Sundried tomatos
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Salt and boil the pasta water
  2. Sear the chicken, salt and pepper to taste. Add garlic and creme fraiche.
  3. Pour down sundried tomatos and let simmer
  4. Mix everything with the pasta and serve
