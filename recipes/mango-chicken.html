Mango chicken

Mango chicken fresh out the oven.

The chicken has been covered in the delicious sauce and it comes out as a tasty dinner.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Mango chutney
  • Heavy cream
  • Orange juice
  • Butter, diced
  • Red curry paste
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil

For serving

  • Pomegranate seeds
  • Peanuts
  • Fresh coriander
  • Fine diced chili
  • Lime
  • Naan bread

Steps

  1. Pre heat the oven to 200 degrees celsius.
  2. Salt and pepper the chicken, sear until golden crust and place is an oven dish.
  3. Mix the remaining ingredients in a mixer, salt and pepper to taste. Pour over the chicken.
  4. Bake in the oven for about 25 minutes, or until the chicken is finished. Before serving, pour over pomegranate seeds, crushed peanuts, coriander and chili.
  5. Don't forget the naan!
