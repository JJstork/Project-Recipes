Homemade pizza

Fresh, savory pizza

A take on the traditional napolitan pizza, fresh and light, great for sharing.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • San marzano tomatos
  • Prosciutto crudo
  • Fresh parmigiano reggiano
  • Buffalo mozzarella
  • Olive oil
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 250 degrees Celsius, or hotter if you can
  2. Form the dough to your liking
  3. Spread the crushed san marzano tomatos on the pizza
  4. Sprinkle parmigiano reggiano on top and pre bake the pizza for about 5 minutes
  5. Add the mozzarella and a little bit more parmigiano reggiano
  6. Once the pizza is finished, take it out and add the prosciutto and dazzle some olive oil on top, serve with fresh basil
